About me.

My name is Sean, I am en epidemiologist working for the UBC and BC Centres for Disease Control in Vancouver, British Columbia. My recent work focuses on COVID-19 variant severity.

My background includes various research roles related to health in academia, industry and volunteer positions, with a focus in methodology, data / statistical analysis and academic writing. I have a passion for research in health sciences as well as data science, specifically data analysis and data visualisaiton.

I currently conduct research related to COVID-19 variant of convern (VOC) severity at the BC Centre for Disease Control (BCCDC). Our work is focused on understanding how SARS-CoV-2 variants of concern impact disease severity and health outcomes using large administrative databases. We are also using SARS-CoV-2 sequencing data to evaluate the impact of other emerging variants.

  • Leading the scientific writing of manuscripts for internal use and for publication in scientific journals
